TED-Ed

Best educational Youtube channels to frequent. Everything from lessons ideas to professional development opportunities and more can be found here. Characterized by its incredible animation style, the stunning videos on TedEd currently have over 12 million subscribers and counting.

What if

What If: These two simple words are the spark that has ignited countless theories, discoveries, and inventions.What If is a documentary web series that takes you on an epic journey through hypothetical worlds and possibilities, some in distant corners of the universe, others right here on Earth.

Big Think

Leading source of expert-driven and actionable educational content. Explore thousands of videos featuring experts discussing topics aimed at getting us to think differently about the big ideas and core skills taht define knowledge in the 21st century.

Khan Academy

You can learn anything on Khan Academy's YouTube channel. As most know, all Khan Academy content is 100% free.

National Geographic

If you saw issues of this amazing and timeless publication in your home growing up, then you know just how strong National Geographic has been going. Their own educational Youtube channels feature content-specific videos on Nag Geo Wild and the super-popular National Geographic Kids.

Crash Course

Launched with the purpose of teaching a wide range of subjects such as world history, biology, and even psychology. With 11.7 million subscritbers and over 1.5 billion views, Crash Course uses a combination of historical footage, descriptive illustrations, and humor to keep viewers informed and entertained.

Vsauce

Created by internet celebrity Michael Stevens. The channels feature videos on scientific, psychological, mathematical, and philosophical topics, as well as gaming, technology, popular culture, and other general interest subjects.

Veritasium

Veritasium has provided an insiders look at science, innovations, and places of interest. Each video includes demonstrations, interviews and/or tours in order to give you a 3-dimensional look at the subject matter.

Easy Languages

If you've always wanted to learn another language, then this is the channel for you! Easy languages has conducted interviews on the streets in order to teach viewers about other people's language and cultures.

Imaginary Ambitions

This channel presents unconventional explorations of what defines different musical genres, styles, and artists. Striving to create original tracks inspired by different styles of music that you will find interesting. Watch the videos for a comedic outline of how the music you're listening to, was made.

Ali Abdaal

Ali Abadaal is a junior doctor in the UK’s National Health Service (NHS). He vlogs about life as a doctor, as well as about how to study more effectively. He makes videos about medicine, tech, productivity and lifestyle. Check out my most popular videos.

Computerphile

Computerphile has provided viewers with all sorts of interesting trivia from the world of computers, plus in-depth explanations about all things tech.

How to Adult

This is a channel that will serve you well for years to come and maybe should've been introduced to you years ago. How to Adult is perfect for those lingering questions of adulthook that you should probably know the answer to but have scooted by for the time being.
